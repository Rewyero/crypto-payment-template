Frequently Asked Questions

How do I integrate CoinFuse with my website?

Our integration process is simple. Start by creating an API key in your dashboard, then follow our getting started guide for step-by-step instructions.

Which cryptocurrencies do you support?

We currently support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and other major cryptocurrencies. Check our API documentation for the full list.

How do refunds work?

Due to the nature of blockchain transactions, refunds must be processed manually. Contact our support team with your transaction ID for assistance.

Is there a fee for using CoinFuse?

We charge a small percentage fee on successful transactions. View our pricing page for detailed information about our fee structure.

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns.
